## Overview

Proof-based number theory needs clear structure. Combine modular arithmetic
with factorization and bounding.

## Key Ideas

- Use order arguments to prove divisibility claims.
- LTE and lifting are powerful for expressions like $a^n-b^n$.
- Check small primes for contradictions.
