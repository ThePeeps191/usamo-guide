## Overview

USAMO is a proof exam. Success depends on strategy, time allocation, and clear
solution organization.

## Key Ideas

- Plan before you write: outline lemmas and the final claim.
- Use headings like "Claim" and "Proof" to structure the write-up.
- Reserve time to review for gaps or unstated assumptions.
