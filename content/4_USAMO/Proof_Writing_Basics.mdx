## Overview

USAMO solutions are written proofs. This module focuses on clarity, structure,
and logically complete arguments.

## Key Ideas

- State what you are proving and define all variables.
- Use complete sentences and justify each step.
- Highlight the key invariant or construction early.

## Worked Example

To prove a statement for all integers $n\ge1$, write a base case and then an
inductive step or a direct argument that covers all cases.
