## Overview

USAMO geometry emphasizes constructions and clean arguments. Inversion and
homography can simplify tough configurations.

## Key Ideas

- Inversion maps lines and circles to lines and circles.
- Projective transformations preserve cross ratios and collinearity.
- Auxiliary points often reveal similarity or power of a point.
