## Overview

Olympiad inequalities require proof structure, not just computation. Learn to
choose substitutions that make known inequalities applicable.

## Key Ideas

- Homogenize expressions to balance degrees.
- Substitute $a=xy$, $b=yz$, $c=zx$ or $a=\frac{x}{y}$ in symmetric problems.
- Identify equality cases to guide transformations.
