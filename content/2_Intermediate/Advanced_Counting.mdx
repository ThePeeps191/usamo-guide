## Overview

Intermediate counting problems demand structure. Learn to avoid overcounting
by using systematic cases and complementary counting.

## Key Ideas

- Stars and bars counts nonnegative solutions to $x_1+\cdots+x_k=n$.
- Inclusion-exclusion fixes overlaps: $|A\cup B|=|A|+|B|-|A\cap B|$.
- Symmetry reduces cases by grouping equivalent configurations.

## Worked Example

How many nonnegative solutions to $x+y+z=10$ with $x\ge2$?

Let $x'=x-2$. Then $x'+y+z=8$ with nonnegative variables.
The answer is $\binom{8+3-1}{3-1}=\binom{10}{2}=45$.
